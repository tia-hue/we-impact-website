Our Mission

Business as a force for lasting good.

Impact is a community dedicated to developing entrepreneurs — the key principles, workshops, scholarships, and servant leadership needed to build businesses that change lives. Everything we do is rooted in four core values: integrity, humility, philanthropy, and community — timeless principles, drawn from our faith and open to everyone.

No. 1 — Equip

Every tool you need

We give entrepreneurs real tools, coaching, and a supportive community — everything needed to lead well and move from idea to action.

No. 2 — Character

Servant leadership

We teach leaders to serve first — business grounded in integrity, humility, and philanthropy, where success lifts everyone it touches.

No. 3 — Purpose

Purpose‑driven decisions

We encourage purpose‑driven decision‑making and values‑filled vision in every area of entrepreneurship.

No. 4 — Inspiration

Learn from real success

Our members share real examples of what worked — successes you can study, borrow from, and apply to your own business.
